## [0.1.0] - 2025-12-15

### Added
- Initial organization-level `.github` repository configuration
- `README.md` with organization overview, featured projects, and contact information
- `CONTRIBUTING.md` with contribution guidelines and eligibility requirements
- `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` establishing community standards and enforcement policies
- `SECURITY.md` with vulnerability reporting process and supported project criteria
- Issue templates: `bug_report.yml` and `feature_request.yml`
- `pull_request_template.md` for standardized PR descriptions across organization repositories
